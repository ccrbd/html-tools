How to use

  1. Choose a difficulty, board theme, clock and which colour to play as, then press New Game.
  2. Tap a piece to see its legal moves (dots), then tap a highlighted square to move.
  3. Castle by selecting your king and tapping two squares toward the rook — or just tap your own rook. Promote a pawn by picking a piece in the pop-up.
  4. Use Fullscreen for a native, distraction-free board. Flip turns the board around, Undo takes back your last move.
  5. Your wins, losses and draws are saved on this device. Captured-piece points show who is ahead.

100% offline — no accounts, no tracking, nothing leaves your browser.
