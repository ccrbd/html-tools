UNO

Match colour or number, fire off Skips, Reverses and Wild Draw Fours, and shout “UNO!” before the bots catch you. Play 1–3 AI opponents across four hand-built themes — entirely offline, on your device.

How to play

  1. Pick your opponents, difficulty and theme, then press Start match.
  2. On your turn, play any card that matches the discard pile’s colour, number or symbol. Glowing cards are legal; dimmed ones aren’t.
  3. Tap a card to play it, or drag it onto the centre pile. Wild cards let you pick the next colour.
  4. If you can’t play, press Draw to take a card from the deck.
  5. When you’re down to one card, hit the big UNO! button within 1.5s — or a bot can catch you for a +2 penalty. Tap it on a careless bot to catch them too.
  6. First to empty their hand wins the round. Turn on Auto-play any time to let the game play your hand for you.

Everything runs locally — no accounts, no network, no tracking. Your last settings are remembered on this device.
