How to use

  1. Pick a difficulty, theme and tile size, then press New Game.
  2. Tap a cell, then tap a number (or use your keyboard 1–9). Same numbers and the active row, column and box are highlighted to help you scan.
  3. Turn on Notes to pencil in candidates. Check flags conflicting numbers in red. Hint fills one correct cell when you're stuck.
  4. Use Pause to hide the board and stop the clock, and Full for a distraction-free fullscreen board. Your game and best times are saved on this device.

100% offline — every puzzle is generated locally and has a single unique solution.
