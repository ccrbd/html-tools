How to use

  1. In the lobby pick your colour, how many bots to face, each bot's difficulty, and a visual theme, then press Start game.
  2. On your turn, tap the dice. Tokens you can move will glow and pulse — tap one to move it.
  3. You need a 6 to bring a token out of its base. Landing on an opponent (off a star/safe square) sends it home.
  4. Rolling a 6 or making a capture earns an extra turn. Three 6s in a row forfeits the turn.
  5. First player to get all four tokens into the centre wins. Your last setup is remembered on this device.
  6. Shortcuts: Space roll · 1–4 move a glowing token · F fullscreen · M music · S sound · N new game. Toggle sound effects and ambient music from the side buttons.

100% offline — no accounts, no tracking, nothing leaves your browser. All sound is generated live in your browser (no audio files), so it stays royalty-free and tiny.
