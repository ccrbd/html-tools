Low Card mig33 revival

The classic chatroom elimination game. Tap the deck before the 20-second timer dies — the player holding the lowest card each round is knocked out. Tie for lowest? Sudden-death redraw. Last survivor takes the pot. You vs 3 bots, fully offline.

How to play

  1. Set your name, theme and pot, then press Enter the table. You play against 3 bots.
  2. Each round a 20-second timer counts down. Tap the deck to draw your card before it hits zero — miss it and the table auto-draws for you.
  3. Once everyone has drawn, all cards flip over at once. Aces are high, 2 is the lowest. Suits don’t matter.
  4. Whoever holds the single lowest card is eliminated. Tie for lowest? Those players enter an instant sudden-death redraw while the safe players watch.
  5. Rounds continue 4 → 3 → 2 players until one survivor wins the pot.
  6. Flip on Auto-play any time to let the table draw for you automatically.

Everything runs locally — no accounts, no network, no tracking. Your last settings are remembered on this device.
