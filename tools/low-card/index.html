How to play

  1. Set your name, table size (4–20 players) and stake, then press Enter the table. Your stake is anted from your balance.
  2. Each round a 20-second timer counts down. Tap the deck to draw before it hits zero — miss it and the table auto-draws for you.
  3. Once everyone has drawn, all cards flip at once. Aces are high, 2 is the lowest. Suits don’t matter.
  4. Whoever holds the single lowest card is eliminated. Tie for lowest? Those players enter an instant sudden-death redraw while the rest watch.
  5. Rounds continue until one survivor wins the whole pot — added straight to your balance.
  6. Knocked out early? Fast-forward the rest in a second or keep watching. Use Auto-play or Fullscreen any time.

Everything runs locally — no accounts, no network, no tracking. Your balance and last settings are remembered on this device.
